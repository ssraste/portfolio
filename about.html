About Me

I grew up in Mumbai, India, where I pursued my bachelors in Electronics Engineering. After Graduation, I moved to Massachusetts to pursue a master’s in Robotics, where I was fortunate to work in research labs and discovered my passion for bioinspired robotics.

I currently live in the beautiful town of Connecticut, and I am a Research Associate at Wesleyan University, working on legged locomotion on granular media.

In my free time, I love playing badminton, singing, and exploring new coffee shops around town.
